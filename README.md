OceanCentinell – Enterprise Port & Offshore Plan

OceanCentinell is a human-governed maritime cyber defense platform designed for U.S. ports, terminals, and offshore operators. It unifies operational risk, incident visibility, and communications integrity into a single executive dashboard and operational workspace.

Overview

OceanCentinell provides a centralized view of maritime cybersecurity and communications health, enabling organizations to monitor, assess, and respond to operational risks in real time while maintaining full human control over all decision-making processes.

Core Capabilities

Communications Monitoring

SatCom link stability and anomaly detection

VHF/UHF guard channel activity monitoring

AIS Integrity

Detection of spoofing indicators

Vessel tracking validation

Risk Engine

Unified enterprise risk scoring

Real-time risk visibility

Geo-Fencing Control

Enforcement of restricted maritime zones

Alert generation on boundary violations

Incident Management

Real-time incident tracking

Operational visibility across systems

Failover Readiness

Monitoring of emergency communication paths

Resilience validation and system readiness

Operational Value

OceanCentinell enables organizations to:

Maintain secure and resilient communications across maritime operations

Execute controlled drills and incident simulations

Enforce geo-fencing policies and restricted zones

Generate compliance-ready audit evidence aligned with U.S. maritime cybersecurity expectations

Architecture Principles

Human-Governed Model
All actions require human validation. No autonomous system execution is permitted.

Single Dashboard Control
Unified operational and executive visibility.

Enterprise Scope
Designed for full organizational deployment within the U.S. Maritime Transportation System (MTS).
